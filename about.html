EMPOWERING OUR
COMMUNITY

One Touch ICT builds skills, creates opportunities and transforms lives through practical training
and mentorship.

Purpose

OUR MISSION, OUR VISION, AND
WHAT DRIVESUS FORWARD

One Touch ICT exists to equip community members with skills to stand on their
own. We believe practical training, honest work, and quiet dignity that comes from
knowing you can provide for yourself and family.

How

FOUR PILLARS OF OUR
WORK IN THE
COMMUNITY

We focus on what matters most. Technical skills that lead to real jobs.
Financial understanding that builds lasting security. Pathways that connect
people to opportunity. And mentorship that walks alongside each person on their journey.

TECHNICAL TRAINING

We teach vocational skills that employers need and communities value. From electrical work to computer literacy, our training prepares people for real employment.

FINANCIAL LITERACY

Understanding money matters. We teach entrepreneurship and financial planning so people can start businesses and build wealth for themselves and their families.

WOMEN'S EMPOWERMENT

Women and single mothers learn tailoring, catering, hairdressing, and trades that open doors. Economic independence changes everything

JOB PLACEMENT

We don't stop at training. We connect graduates with apprenticeships, mentorships, and employment through partnerships with local industries and TVET institutions.
